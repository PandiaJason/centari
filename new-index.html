Why Choose centari?

Inspired by the Alpha Centauri star system, centari represents the next frontier of education technology, creating software that moves beyond conventional learning platforms.

With enterprise-grade customized Learning Suite infrastructure, secure assessment engines, and scalable digital learning solutions, centari empowers institutions to build the future of education.

★
centari is built with the sole vision to enable everyone to customize their learning management suite in this AI era.
1

Full Digital Classroom at Minimal Cost

Most LMS platforms charge per-student monthly fees that compound fast. centari's Starter Plan bundles a complete MCQ Exam Suite, YouTube Unlisted video delivery, and a live whiteboard classroom — all on a dedicated server starting at just ₹8,600/month infrastructure cost with zero per-user charges.

2

Lightweight Vector-Drawing Whiteboard

Traditional screensharing eats massive bandwidth and looks blurry. centari syncs live whiteboard strokes using mathematical vector coordinates, enabling zero-latency drawing that looks crystal-clear even on slow mobile connections.

3

White-Label Private Cloud Rollout

Instead of locking your data onto a shared vendor SaaS, centari is deployed directly to your private cloud server. You retain complete database authority, custom branding integration, and unlimited scaling without per-user subscription fees.
